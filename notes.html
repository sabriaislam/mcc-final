Field Notes

two stores · two worlds · one quiet revolution

from the visit

Le Labo

  • Open energy from the very first second
  • Feels like a workshop you're allowed to touch everything in
  • Your bottle stamped with your name, city, today's date
  • Samples = a casual gift, no conditions
  • Strong seasonal rotation, leans citrus
  • Genuinely conversational from the jump

Diptyque

  • Formal at first — warm once you're honest
  • You feel the painter-founders everywhere
  • Ink, tree rings, fireplace — scent as art object
  • Samples = a curated feedback exchange
  • Controlled, considered, elegant in its restraint
  • The best version of a formal interaction

What they share

  • No celebrity. No viral moment. Just the scent.
  • Quieter buzz than mass campaigns — and stickier
  • Status signaled through space and restraint, not logos
  • You leave feeling like an insider. That's the product.

Personal take

  • Le Labo won on vibe — immediately open
  • Diptyque's best moment: when honesty arrived
  • Both gave samples, totally different framing
  • Would genuinely return to both
